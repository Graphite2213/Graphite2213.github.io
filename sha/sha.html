Sheer Heart Attack

Invite

Sheer heart attack is, at it's essence, a stand fight simulator where you get to use abilities and items from the JoJo universe. You can fight to earn money to buy arrows to get better stands to win more fights and repeat. I also added some easter eggs that are super fucking hard to find so good luck with obtaining that easter egg hunter badge.

The bot served as NodeJS practice at first but then grew and now it's basically my full time job. The bot gets regular updates and patches to make your experience as amazing as possible. Right now SHA has over 700 users and is in over 400 servers.
